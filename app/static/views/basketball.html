Meet the Onyx Motion Smartwatch

Onyx Motion


We accelerate the process of learning by turning your practice into perfect practice.

We measure your motion and overlay it with a professional’s. The algorithm goes in and decides what you’re doing wrong, just like a coach. After your shot, you swipe to get the insight; from timing of your release to arc of the ball.

When you try again, the score on the screen means you can check to see if your intuition is correct right away and improve every single time.

Onyx Motion helps you pick up your game.
