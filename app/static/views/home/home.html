We're making it easier for you to know your game.

The Onyx Smartwatch compares your technique to the professionals and gives you helpful tips for improving.

By collecting your stats over time, we can paint you a picture of your progress and give you an idea of how to make the best of your practice time.


How it works

Form

Decide what you want to learn. Is it a basketball free throw, a tennis backhand or a golf drive? We've got it all in the Motion Marketplace


Feedback

We compare your technique to a professional athelete. Then, we can show you exactly what you did and what part of your form needs work instantly on the screen

Insight

What time of day do you peak at? How long should your practices be? Are you improving faster than your friends? We collect that information into the Portal phone app. Data is power.
